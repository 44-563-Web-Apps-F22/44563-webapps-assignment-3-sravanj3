Saola

The saola (Pseudoryx nghetinhensis), also called spindlehorn, Asian unicorn, or infrequently, Vu Quang bovid, is one of the world's rarest large mammals, a forest-dwelling bovine native to the Annamite Range in Vietnam and Laos.

Fun Facts About Saola

    >
  1. The Saola is also called the Asian Unicorn.
  2. Sometimes the Saola is called a spindle-horn. This is because its horns look like the spinning wheels of the villagers vehicles.
  3. Sometimes the Saola is called the Quang Vu ox. They live only in the Quang Vu Nature Reserve.
