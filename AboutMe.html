Jai Lava Kusa

The film follows identical triplets, Jai, Lava and Kusa, who get separated at a young age and follow different paths in life. Jai becomes an evil criminal, Kusa becomes a small-time crook and Lava becomes a bank manager. Jai, the eldest of identical triplets was sidelined at a very young age due to stammering.
